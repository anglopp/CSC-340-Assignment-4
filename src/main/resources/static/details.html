Goobster: The Furry Chaos Machine

Small, fluffy and energtic cousins to Remy from Ratatouille, hamsters are known for their adorableness.
Even rat haters like these little guys. Hamsters are also master escapists.

Some Fun Facts:

  • - Hamsters are nocturnal, being most active during the night. Good luck falling asleep.
  • - They can stuff 20% of their body weight in food into their mouths.
  • - Thanks to the shape of their hind feet, they can run backwards.
  • - No hamster dies a normal death, there's always a twist to it.
  • - Life expentancy with good care is about 2-3 years.
